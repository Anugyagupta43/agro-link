FAQs

Q: How do I add a product?

A:Use the product form in your dashboard to add your items.

Q: How do I track orders?

Check the "Order Summary" section to view order details.

Q: How do I manage my orders?

A: All your orders are displayed in the "Order Summary" section of your dashboard. Here, you can see the buyer's name, product details, quantity ordered, and the delivery deadline.

Q: Can I update the price or quantity of a product?

A: Yes, you can edit the details of any product from the "Manage Your Products" section. Simply locate the product you want to update, make changes, and save them.

Q: How do I know if someone has placed an order?

A: You will receive a notification on your dashboard whenever a buyer places an order. The order details will be visible in your "Order Summary" section.

Q: What should I do if I can’t fulfill an order?

A: If you cannot fulfill an order, please contact the buyer directly using the information provided in the order summary. Transparency helps maintain trust and build good relationships with buyers.

Q: Can I sell multiple types of products?

A: Yes, you can list as many products as you want, including fruits, vegetables, dairy products, and more. Each product can have its own price and quantity.
